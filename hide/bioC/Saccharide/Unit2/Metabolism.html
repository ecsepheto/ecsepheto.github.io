Metabolism

The sum of chemical reactions that occurs within each living organism's cell. It provides energy for organisms to live, grow and reproduce.

Metabolic Pathway

Series of Chemical Reactions in a cell, each reaction is catalyzed by an enzyme protein.

Catabolism

Break down polymers (large molecules) into monomers (the smaller subunits) to produce Energy
    Ex: Hydrolysis
    Glycogen breaks down into glucoses

Anabolism

Build polymers from monomers by using Energy.
    Ex: Condensation
    Photosynthesis: CO2+H2O+Energy->Glucose+O2

Glucose:

Glucose has a significant role in Metabolism. In Animal, glucose undergoes glycolysis and provide ATP. In Plants, they synthesizes Glucose to build cellulose.
