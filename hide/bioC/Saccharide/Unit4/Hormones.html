Hormone

Endocrine Hormones works slowly with long-term effect
  • Affects Broad range of cell
  • Communicates Regulatory Message, only affect on target Cells.

    • Terms:

    • Source: where Hormone Chemicals are produced
    • Sink: The parts of body being affected

    Hormone Types:

  • Peptide Hormone
    • Short chain of Amino Acid
    • Can't penetrate membrane
    • Requires Receptors to cause cascade of reactions in the target cell
  • Steroid Hormone
    • Ring Structure
    • Lipid soluable, so penetratable through membrane
    • Binds to the receptor Protein and activate specific genes.

    • Hypothalamus
