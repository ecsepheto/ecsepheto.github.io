Homeostasis

The variables (BloodSugar, BloodPressure, Temperature) inside our body must stay in a approporate range.
Homeostasis is the process to maintain consistent internal environment.

Control Components:

  • Monitor
      Detect change in variables
  • Cooridinating Center
      Receives message from Monitor and directs response via Regulator
  • Regulator
      Carry out response from Cooridinating Center

    • Hypothalamus

    Part of our brain that initiates hormonal/nervous response after receives message from Monitor.

    Feedback System

    Maintains changes in variable

      Negative Feedback

      Change in Environment triggers reaction to reverse the change

      Positive Feedback

      Change in Environment triggers reaction to reinforce the change.
