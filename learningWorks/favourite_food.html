Egg Fried Rice

recipe by Diw

A Egg Fried Rice is a no-fuss meal, that you can easily modify with whatever vegetables you have on hand.



Ingridents:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons peanut, vegetable, or olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced scallions, white and green parts divided
  • pinch of salt
  • 4 cups cooked overnight jasmine rice
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and beat them together
  2. Add the soy sauce, five-spice powder, dash of white pepper (if using) and sesame oil and stir to distribute the seasonings. Add the scrambled eggs and stir to mix again. Garnish with remaining sliced scallions. Serve immediately.
  3. Drizzle the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil into a wok over high heat. Add the onions and cook them for about 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the mixed vegetables and white parts of the scallions and cook for 2 minutes. Season the vegetables with a pinch of salt.
  4. Add the cooked rice into the pan and cook for several minutes, until the rice is heated through. If there are large clumps of rice, break them apart with the back of your spatula.
  5. Add the soy sauce, five-spice powder and sesame oil and stir to distribute the seasonings. Add the scrambled eggs and stir to mix again. Garnish with remaining sliced scallions. Serve immediately.
